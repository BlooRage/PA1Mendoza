Dream places to visit in the Philippines by order
Place Something about it Image
Batanes I'm seriously drawn to Batanes! The thought of exploring its stunning mountain ranges, soaking in the breathtaking scenery, and immersing myself in its rich heritage just gets me so excited. And let's not forget those iconic stone houses and the romantic idea of beautiful mountain weddings. t truly feels like a place where time slows down, offering a unique and unforgettable escape.
Palawan "The stunning beaches, islands, and crystal clear waters of Palawan never cease to amaze me. Did you know that Palawan alone has over 1,700 islands? That’s one of the reasons why I’ve always wanted to visit Palawan; it’s truly a tropical paradise waiting to be explored.
Pinatubo Crater The beautiful devastation of Mount Pinatubo’s crater makes me want to go and see it for myself. It’s amazing how a place born from such a powerful eruption can now be so peaceful and breathtaking. The contrast between its violent history and serene landscape is something I’ve always found fascinating. I’ve heard that the trail going to the crater involves a scenic 4x4 ride and a trek across rocky paths and lahar fields; the adventure itself sounds just as rewarding as the destination
Sagada Sagada is located in the Mountain Province in the Cordillera Region of northern Luzon. It’s a quiet mountain town known for its hanging coffins, limestone caves, rice terraces, and cool climate. Just hearing about all of that makes me want to go there and experience its beauty and culture for myself.
Las Casas Filipinas De Acuzar "Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar in Bagac, Bataan is one of the dream beach resorts I’ve always wanted to visit. With its beautiful black sand beach and remarkable touch of Filipino heritage, it doesn’t just offer a once-in-a-lifetime beach experience; it also immerses you in the lifestyle, culture, and history of being a true heritage Filipino. Walking through its cobblestone streets and restored ancestral houses feels like traveling back in time it is a perfect blend of relaxation and historical appreciation.
Amsterdam, Netherlands Amsterdam, Netherlands is definitely one of the places I dream of visiting. From its charming canals and historic buildings to its vibrant bike culture and art museums, the city seems full of life and beauty. I’ve always wanted to see the colorful tulip fields, ride a bike along the canals, and visit the famous Van Gogh Museum. There’s something so peaceful yet exciting about Amsterdam that makes me want to experience it all in person.
Japan Japan is one of the countries I’ve always wanted to visit. From the serene beauty of cherry blossoms and ancient temples to the fast-paced life of cities like Tokyo and Osaka, it’s a perfect mix of tradition and modernity. I want to experience the culture firsthand — wear a kimono, walk through Kyoto’s old streets, and enjoy authentic Japanese food like ramen, sushi, and takoyaki. I’m also fascinated by their discipline, cleanliness, and deep respect for nature and tradition. Visiting Japan would truly be a dream come true.
France France, especially Paris, is one of my ultimate dream destinations. The romantic streets, iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, and charming cafés make it feel like a place straight out of a movie. I want to stroll along the Seine River, explore the art at the Louvre, and enjoy fresh croissants at a cozy Parisian bakery. Paris isn’t just about beauty; it is also rich in history, fashion, and culture that I’ve always admired. Just thinking about it makes me want to pack my bags and go.
Greece Greece is one of the places I’ve always dreamed of visiting. With its whitewashed houses, stunning blue waters, and ancient ruins, it looks like a postcard come to life. I want to explore the historical sites in Athens, relax on the beaches of Santorini, and try authentic Greek food like souvlaki and moussaka. There’s something magical about how Greece blends mythology, history, and natural beauty. Just thinking about it makes me want to book a flight and experience it all myself.
Maldives The Maldives is definitely on my travel bucket list. With its overwater villas, crystal clear turquoise waters, and white sandy beaches, it looks like absolute paradise. I’ve always wanted to swim with vibrant marine life, relax by the sea, and just enjoy the peaceful, luxurious vibe the islands are known for. It seems like the perfect place to unwind and escape from the noise of everyday life. Visiting the Maldives would be a dream come true for me.
